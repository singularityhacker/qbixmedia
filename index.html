About

My name is Justice Gödel Conder and I started Qbix Media in 2009. Qbix Media started out as a domain reseller and hosting service but quickly grew to encompass many other Internet and tech oriented projects and services. The company's primary focus now is threefold: consultation, front-end web development, and advertising. In addition to my own expertise, Qbix Media relies on a network of technical professionals to provide its clients with the most accurate and up-to-date information and services related to emerging technologies.

Services

Work





Bitcoin Vending Machine

Bitcoin Vending Machine is a proof-of-concept website exploring the possiblity of monitizing online file sharing with the cryptocurrency, Bitcoin. No files are hosted by the site and users do not need a torrent client installed to download files. Note: The full functionality of this site has been temporarily suspended to prevent intellectual property violations and for liability reasons related to Qbix Media.

Blitzcall

The Blitzcall website demonstrates how a legacy website and business model can be brought up to date. All hardcopy books were converted to digital downloads. An automated subscription based newsletter and ticket sales functionality were added to the updated website as well. All branding and digital ebook designs were done by Qbix Media. All books are pending approval from Apple for the iBook store.

iFlashYou

iFlashYou is the result of a joint venture between Qbix Media and Nostalgic Productions. iFlashYou is a company based out of Orlando Florida specializing in high-end audio/video work and flash based web design.

Easel For iPad

This is the perfect example of a profitable minimal-viable-product website. Leveraging market forces and principles of consumer psychology, Qbix Media launched a successful product within twenty four hours of the release of the Apple iPad.

Truax Publishing

Truax Publishing is a digital publishing platform for shorter form books that takes advantage of Twitter and Facebook. User can either purchase their ebooks outright or download them for free by mentioning the title in a tweet liking it on Facebook. This project is still in the early phases of development. All logo design, branding, and digital book cover design was done by Qbix Media.
